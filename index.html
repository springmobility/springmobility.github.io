DEFINING A NEW GENERATION OF MOBILITY AIDS, TOGETHER.

Imagine a world in which mobility aids are more than functional; a world in which they are customizable, adaptable, and an expression of who you are.

It's time for young mobility aid users' to have their voices heard by the Assistive Technology (AT) industry. You deserve mobility aids that enable you, and are built with your needs in mind.

COMFORT. CONFIDENCE. CUSTOM.

Designed to empower, SPRING mobility aids provide the support you need to move with comfort and confidence. Our customizable aids can adapt to every occasion; from use around the home, to a formal party, and anywhere else life takes you. With an emphasis on modularity, SPRING mobility aids will give you the choice to swap out handles and cane tips, to give you the grip and support you need, when you need it.

IT'S TIME TO EXPECT MORE.

Our mission is to work with the disabled community to make the products that you want, the way you want them. To do this, we need to collaborate with you to discover what the future of mobility aids can and should be.
