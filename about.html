SPRING Mobility was founded to challenge the Assistive Technology (AT) Industry

The SPRING Mobility team is determined to change the way young mobility aid users interact with and use their AT. Founded by three students out of Canada, we hope to draw from our experience in engineering design to create AT that users can be proud of.
